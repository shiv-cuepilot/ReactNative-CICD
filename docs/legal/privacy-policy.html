Privacy Policy — IgniteKit

Last updated: 16 July 2026 · Educators Labs Private Limited · support@educatorslabs.com

This Privacy Policy explains what information the IgniteKit app (the “App”) handles and how. We designed IgniteKit to collect as little as possible.

1. Information we do not collect

We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, precise location, or advertising identifiers. We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for advertising.

2. Information handled automatically

  • App update checks. The App checks whether a newer version is available. On iOS it queries Apple’s public App Store lookup service and, for over-the-air updates, our updates provider (Expo Application Services); these requests include your device OS version, app version, and update runtime identifier. On Android it uses Google Play’s in-app update service.
  • Diagnostics. Basic non-identifying technical details (device model, OS version, app version) may be processed to improve stability. This is not linked to your identity.
  • Local storage. Some settings may be stored only on your device and never leave it unless described above.

3. Third-party services

  • Expo Application Services (EAS Update / OTA) — expo.dev/privacy
  • Apple App Store — apple.com/legal/privacy
  • Google Play services — policies.google.com/privacy

4. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Data retention

We do not maintain user profiles. Technical/diagnostic data is retained only as long as needed and then discarded or aggregated.

6. Your rights

Because we do not collect personal data tied to your identity, there is typically no personal data to access, correct, or delete. Questions: support@educatorslabs.com.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
