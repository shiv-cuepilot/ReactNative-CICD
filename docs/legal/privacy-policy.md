# Privacy Policy — SnapBiodata

**Last updated:** 16 July 2026
**Publisher:** SnapBiodata ("we", "us", "our")
**App:** SnapBiodata (the "App")
**Contact:** support@snapbiodata.com · https://snapbiodata.com

This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles and how. We designed
SnapBiodata to collect as little as possible.

## 1. Information we do not collect

We do **not** ask you to create an account, and we do **not** collect your name,
email address, phone number, contacts, photos, precise location, or advertising
identifiers. We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for
advertising.

## 2. Information handled automatically

To operate the App and keep it up to date, limited technical information is
processed:

- **App update checks.** The App checks whether a newer version is available.
  - On iOS, it queries Apple's public App Store lookup service and, for
    over-the-air (OTA) updates, contacts our updates provider (Expo
    Application Services). These requests include your device's operating system
    version, app version, and update runtime identifier.
  - On Android, it uses Google Play's in-app update service, which is governed by
    Google's own terms and privacy policy.
- **Diagnostics.** If the App crashes or errors, basic non-identifying technical
  details (device model, OS version, app version) may be processed to improve
  stability. This is not linked to your identity.
- **Local storage.** Some settings may be stored only on your device. This data
  never leaves your device unless described above.

## 3. Third-party services

The App relies on the following services, each with its own privacy policy:

- **Expo Application Services (EAS Update / OTA)** — https://expo.dev/privacy
- **Apple App Store** — https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
- **Google Play services (in-app updates)** — https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 4. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect
personal information from children.

## 5. Data retention

We do not maintain user profiles. Technical/diagnostic data described above is
retained only as long as needed for the purpose it was processed and then
discarded or aggregated.

## 6. Your rights

Because we do not collect personal data tied to your identity, there is typically
no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you have any question about
your privacy, contact us at support@snapbiodata.com.

## 7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by
updating the "Last updated" date above.
